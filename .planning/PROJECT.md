# SmartOps AI Project

## What This Is
SmartOps AI is an agentic AI platform designed to automate ticket triage, knowledge management, and customer operations for modern support teams.

## Core Value
Zero-latency automated triage and RAG-grounded response drafting that keeps humans in the loop.
